Facilitation and Note-Taking Techniques

All successful meetings have an agenda with a clear purpose, follow a concrete process, and are hosted by a competent and confident facilitator. You'll gain the knowledge, skills, and attitude to facilitate any kind of meeting using a variety of meeting methods and technologies. All successful meetings also have a clear, concise, and complete minutes-of-meeting ensuring of appropriate roles, responsibilities, clearly communicated to all concerned. You'll follow the process of planning, drafting, re-drafting, proofreading, and final writing to make sure you provide a true, impartial, and balanced account of the proceedings.

Following an introductory video-call or face-to-face meeting to understand your requirements, we'll decide what I can do to help you make your meetings successful.
