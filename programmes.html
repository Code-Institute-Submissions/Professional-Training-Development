Foundations in IT Project Management

A project is a temporary endeavour that has a definite beginning and end. It results in a unique product, service, or result. A successful project accomplishes the goals agreed with the client stakeholders. We cover the five process groups of the PMBOK Guide: Initiating, Planning, Executing, Monitoring and Controlling, and Closing. Working on either an IT Project of your choice or one of many course examples, you will work on concepts such as defining requirements, creating the project charter and scope documents, planning the project, assessing and managing risk, and closing out the project.

This course a 5-day instructor-led course, though is easily configured to suit your corporate requirements.
